Verified facility details

Verified: 2026-01-29

Hours: Tuesdays/Wednesdays 9 a.m.–3 p.m.; 2nd/4th Saturdays 9 a.m.–2 p.m. (by appointment)
Fees: Free for Harris County residents (outside Houston city services)
Rules: Harris County residents only (no City of Houston services/taxes); appointment required; enter at Hahl Rd (290/Gessner).
Accepted:
  • Aerosols (no Freon/mace)
  • Automotive products (gas, oil)
  • Batteries (up to car size)
  • Household cleaners
  • Chemicals (pool, fertilizers, pesticides)
  • Propane cylinders
  • Tires (up to 5/visit)
  • Thermometers/mercury items
Not accepted:
  • Commercial hazardous
  • Medical waste
