Verified facility details

Verified: 2026-01-29

Hours: Tuesday–Saturday: 8 a.m.–5 p.m.
Fees: Free for residents (up to 4 visits/month)
Rules: Houston residents only; proof required (TX ID + utility bill/lease/tax bill/student ID); arrive 30 min before closing; no commercial vehicles.
Accepted:
  • Household trash
  • Yard waste
  • Bulk items
  • Recyclables (plastics, paper, glass, metals)
  • Up to 5 tires/month (no rims)
  • Used motor oil
  • Tree waste
  • Cardboard
Not accepted:
  • Commercial waste
  • Hazardous materials (except at designated centers)
  • Rubber materials (tubes, hoses, gloves)
  • Extension cords
  • Wiring/string lights
